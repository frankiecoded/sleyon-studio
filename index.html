Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and what to expect during your session. If you have more questions, don’t hesitate to reach out—we’re here to help!

What services does Sleyon Studios offer?

Sleyon Studios is a fully equipped media house in Nairobi, Kenya, bringing together a photo studio, video studio, and music studio under one roof. We specialize in professional photography, video production, and audio recording, making us your go-to space for all media needs. Whether you're an artist looking to record your next hit, a couple wanting memorable wedding photos, or a brand in need of promotional videos, our team has the expertise to deliver high-quality, impactful results.

Do you offer packages for events like weddings, corporate shoots, or private sessions?

Absolutely! We know that every event is unique, so we offer tailored packages for a variety of occasions, including weddings, corporate events, fashion shoots, family portraits, and more. Our team is committed to capturing every special moment, combining creativity with technical excellence. Let us know your vision, and we'll work with you to create a package that fits your needs and budget, ensuring you get the best value and results.

Where is Sleyon Studios located?

We’re located in Nairobi, right at the heart of Kenya’s vibrant media and creative scene. Our studio’s central location makes it convenient for clients coming from all over Nairobi and even from other parts of Kenya. Reach out to us for directions or to schedule a visit to explore our facilities and discuss your project in person.

What kind of equipment and technology do you use?

At Sleyon Studios, we believe that great work starts with great tools. Our studios are equipped with industry-standard cameras, lighting setups, sound equipment, and editing software to ensure every project is polished and professional. We invest in the latest technology to provide you with crisp, clear visuals and high-fidelity audio, whether it’s for a photoshoot, a video production, or a recording session.

How do I book a session at Sleyon Studios?

Booking a session with us is straightforward and convenient. You can book by visiting our Contact page, giving us a call, or messaging us on social media. Our team will guide you through the booking process, help you choose the right service or package, and answer any questions you might have about preparing for your session. We’re here to make the experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

What makes Sleyon Studios different from other studios in Nairobi?

Sleyon Studios isn’t just a studio; it’s a creative hub where ideas come to life. By bringing photography, videography, and music production together, we offer a unique, versatile space where creatives can collaborate, innovate, and express themselves fully. Our team is passionate about storytelling and dedicated to delivering high-quality, impactful content that resonates with Kenyan audiences and beyond. We’re here to turn your vision into reality with style and professionalism.
